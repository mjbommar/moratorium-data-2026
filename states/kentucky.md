## Detailed entries

### Breckinridge County

- **Type:** County
- **Date enacted:** 2025-12-05
- **Duration:** 365 days
- **Legal basis:** Ordinance 2025-1117A (AI Data Center Moratorium)
- **What prompted it:** Concerns about AI and data center facility impacts; need for regulatory framework
- **Status:** Active through approximately December 2026
- **Affected projects:** Not specified in ordinance text
- **Outcome:** Pending development of permanent regulations

### City of La Grange

- **Type:** City
- **Date enacted:** 2025-06-02
- **Duration:** 150 days initially; extension ordinance identified but final term needs confirma
- **Legal basis:** Ordinance No. 10, Series 2025; Ordinance No. 17-2025 listed as "An Ordinance Extending Moratorium"
- **What prompted it:** Increased data center interest in Oldham County; infrastructure, energy, land-use, environmental, and regulatory-gap concerns
- **Status:** Extended by Ordinance 17-2025 per City of La Grange ordinance index. Final extension term and current expiration not confirmed in captured records
- **Affected projects:** Project Lincoln / OC Data Center area proposals; new applications paused, filed applications exempted
- **Outcome:** City ordinance list confirms a moratorium extension, but final signed text was not captured

### Meade County Fiscal Court

- **Type:** County
- **Date enacted:** 2026-01-13
- **Duration:** 12 months / 365 days, unless terminated earlier or extended
- **Legal basis:** Ordinance No. 2025-07
- **What prompted it:** County zoning ordinance and comprehensive plan did not specifically address data center development; need to review infrastructure, environmental, and land-use compatibility impacts
- **Status:** Active; expected to run until 2027-01-13 unless replaced or extended
- **Affected projects:** Meade County data center / IT infrastructure applications generally; no specific paused project confirmed in captured primary text
- **Outcome:** Planning and Zoning Commission directed to evaluate impacts, zoning districts, performance standards, and text amendments

### Oldham County Fiscal Court

- **Type:** County
- **Date enacted:** 2025-06-26 (per Oldham Era public notice scheduling second reading/public hearing/adoption for that date); signed-ordinance date with vote tally not separately retrieved
- **Duration:** 150 days initially; later extended until data center regulations are approved
- **Legal basis:** Ordinance KOC 25-910-508; extension KOC 25-910-520
- **What prompted it:** Increased data center siting interest; infrastructure, utility capacity, energy use, land use, and environmental sustainability concerns; zoning rules did not adequately address data centers
- **Status:** Active as of 2026-03-26 reporting
- **Affected projects:** Project Lincoln / OC Data Center proposal near La Grange; reporting describes a withdrawn proposal after intense public pushback
- **Outcome:** Proposed permanent regulations were remanded for more work in January 2026; the moratorium remained in effect during revisions
