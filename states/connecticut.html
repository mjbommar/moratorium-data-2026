Detailed entries

Groton

  • Type: Town
  • Status: ⚪ Replaced
  • Date enacted: 2022-06-14 (Groton PZC adoption per captured agenda packet); effective 2022-07-1
  • Duration: One year
  • Moratorium ID: ct-groton-2022
  • Legal basis: Planning and Zoning Commission application REGA22-01, Data Center Moratorium Text Amendment, town-wide
  • What prompted it: Local proposals for large data centers, uncertainty about how data centers differed from telecommunications facilities, and resident concerns about noise, stormwater, generator use, energy use, and environmental impacts
  • Affected projects: Data center applications; public reporting states the pause applied to developments larger than 5,000 square feet and followed proposals for two 250,000-square-foot facilities near Interstate 95
  • Detailed status: Replaced
  • Outcome: Permanent regulations adopted in 2023 limit principal data centers to 12,500 square feet and impose siting and operating limits; public reporting describes this as blocking hyperscale projects

Morris

  • Type: Town
  • Status: 🟡 Pending
  • Date enacted: 2026-03-23 document date; the captured text reads as a draft scheduled for PZC v
  • Duration: 12 months from effective date of adoption
  • Moratorium ID: ct-morris-2026
  • Legal basis: Town of Morris Planning and Zoning Commission, Zoning Moratorium on Battery Energy Storage Systems and Data Centers
  • What prompted it: Need to evaluate land use, environmental, infrastructure, fire safety, emergency response, groundwater, traffic, noise, visual, electrical infrastructure, and water-use impacts
  • Affected projects: Applications for establishment, construction, installation, or expansion of battery energy storage systems or data centers; no named project identified
  • Detailed status: Status unverified; document is dated 2026-03-23 and reads as a moratorium proposed for PZC adoption
  • Outcome: Pending; moratorium directs the commission to prepare zoning amendments as appropriate
