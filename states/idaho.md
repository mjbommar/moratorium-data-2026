## Detailed entries

### Kootenai County

- **Type:** County
- **Date enacted:** 2025-03-11, retroactive to 2025-02-27
- **Duration:** 182 days from 2025-03-11 adoption; nominal expiration approximately 2025-09-09 (
- **Legal basis:** Resolution 2025-23, "Emergency Moratorium - Data Centers"
- **What prompted it:** Developer interest in unincorporated county parcels over the Rathdrum Prairie Aquifer; county findings cited water quantity and quality, electricity demand, air pollution, building size, noise, parking, and traffic circulation.
- **Status:** Expired and replaced
- **Affected projects:** No named project or MW total identified in captured county records. County records and press coverage state only that developers had made inquiries.
- **Outcome:** Ordinance 620, noticed 2025-10-07, amended county code to prohibit data centers in the Commercial zone and allow them in Light Industrial and Industrial zones only with a conditional use permit.
