## Detailed entries

### Montour County

- **Type:** County
- **Date enacted:** 2025-11-18
- **Duration:** 180 days, expiring about 2026-05-17 unless extended or superseded
- **Legal basis:** Resolution R-11-19-2025
- **What prompted it:** Concern over potential data centers or similar large-scale digital/technology facilities; county needed time for Planning Commission ad-hoc committee ordinance work
- **Status:** Active as of 2026-04-29; nominal expiration approximately 2026-05-17 (180 days from 2025-11-18). Replacement Data Center Land Use Ordinance noticed for April 2026 public hearing per Montour County records
- **Affected projects:** No pending data center applications at enactment; Talen Energy later sought rezoning of Anthony Township parcels for a data-center-related industrial use
- **Outcome:** County denied Talen Energy's zoning map amendment on 2026-02-12 and moved toward a Data Center Land Use Ordinance that would allow data centers by conditional use in the Industrial Zoning District
