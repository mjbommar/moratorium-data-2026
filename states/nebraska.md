## Detailed entries

### Hitchcock County

- **Type:** County
- **Date enacted:** 2025-02-03
- **Duration:** One year or until updated zoning regulations were adopted, whichever was less
- **Legal basis:** Resolution #2025-7, Temporary Moratorium on Data Centers and Crypto Mining Facilities
- **What prompted it:** Existing zoning did not adequately address data centers and crypto mining in AG-G and RCI districts. The resolution cited energy consumption, cooling and environmental footprint concerns, land-use impacts, and the need to study countywide zoning controls.
- **Status:** Replaced
- **Affected projects:** Steven R. Fries data mining conditional use request was denied on 2025-01-21 before the moratorium. MB Club LLC later received conditional use approval for a data center on 2025-10-06
- **Outcome:** Resolution #25-9 amended county zoning after a 2025-03-17 public hearing. The framework allows data centers and crypto mining through conditional use in AG-G and RCI districts and addresses I district standards. Resolution #25-19 approved MB Club LLC's conditional use with an impact easement require

### Otoe County
